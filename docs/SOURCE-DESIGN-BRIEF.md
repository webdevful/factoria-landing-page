## Source Facts

- Header/topbar: source uses a white contact top bar above a light navigation band with a logo, uppercase menu items, dropdowns, and a quote button.
- Hero: source uses an Owl Carousel slider with slides: "Advanced technology", "Advanced Machinery", background images `banner/13.jpg`, `banner/4.jpg`. Rebuilt as Swiper.
- About: source has a photo on the left with an overlay card "25 Years Work experience in construction", founder signature, and background shape `shape/5.svg`.
- Working with Us CTA: source has an offset section "Contractors & Construction Managers Since 1989", quote CTA, and counter items: "Ongoing Projects" (6), "End Project" (12), "Upcomming Project" (4).
- Services: source services area features 3-column service cards with hover effects and detailed descriptions. Rebuilt as a Swiper carousel.
- Work Process: 3-step grid representing Design 01, Planning 02, and Devolepment 03.
- Portfolio: 2-column latest work project gallery showing 4 projects with hover overlay controls. Rebuilt as a Swiper carousel.
- Team: 3-column team cards showing team members (Jessica Jones, Munia Ankor, Ahmed Kamal). Rebuilt as a Swiper carousel.
- Testimonials: testimonial carousel showing client quotes (Jones Adhor, Mones Basel) with thumbnail images. Rebuilt as a Swiper carousel.
- Blog: 3-column blog grid with meta fields and bold headings.
- Footer: subscription box "Get Subscribed!", social icons, and contact address California, TX 70240.

## Intentional Adaptations

- Source photos were replaced with local Unsplash photographic assets.
- Source logo was replaced by WLFS Forge Line identity.
- Source jQuery/Owl Carousel runtime was modernized to Swiper while preserving slide counts, loop behavior, navigation/dots, and responsive visible counts.
- Source Owl dots and arrows are globally styled to match the generated runtime DOM.
